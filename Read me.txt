# Loan Approval Prediction & Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project is a complete Loan Approval Prediction and Analytics Dashboard built using Power BI and Excel datasets. The dashboard helps analyze customer loan applications, approval trends, applicant profiles, credit history, income distribution, and risk patterns to support better business decisions.

The project demonstrates data cleaning, data visualization, business intelligence reporting, KPI analysis, and interactive dashboard development.

---

# Objectives
- Analyze loan applicant data
- Understand loan approval and rejection patterns
- Identify important factors affecting loan approval
- Create interactive business dashboards
- Generate insights for decision-making

# Key Insights
- Applicants with good credit history have higher approval rates.
- Urban area applicants show higher loan approval percentages.
- Applicant income directly impacts loan approval probability.
- Educated applicants tend to receive more approvals.
- Loan rejection is higher among applicants with poor credit history.
